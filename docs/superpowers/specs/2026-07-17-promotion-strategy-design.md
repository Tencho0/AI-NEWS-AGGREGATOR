# Design — Promotion strategy: reaching the largest possible audience

**Date:** 2026-07-17 · **Status:** Approved (pending spec review) · **Related:**
docs/01-vision-and-scope.md, docs/superpowers/specs/2026-07-17-facebook-engagement-design.md,
docs/10-roadmap.md, docs/adr/0008-facebook-page-only.md, docs/adr/0009-image-sourcing-rules.md

## Problem

Predel News has a working publish pipeline (site + Facebook Page) but essentially no growth
engine: Facebook posts get little reach (being fixed by the 2026-07-17 engagement design), the
site gets no search/Discover traffic, under-35s are unreached, and there is no measurement.
The owner wants a strategy to reach the largest possible audience.

## Owner decisions (2026-07-17)

- **Deliverable:** both a marketing playbook (human actions) *and* a prioritized growth-feature
  roadmap (pipeline work).
- **Budget:** organic only — zero ad spend.
- **Audience focus:** region first — own Blagoevgrad / Southwest Bulgaria before anything else.
- **New channels approved:** Instagram. Short vertical video (TikTok / YouTube Shorts / Reels)
  was initially approved but **deferred on 2026-07-18** — not implemented for now; revisit after
  the Instagram leg and SEO pack are live. Viber and a public Telegram channel were considered
  and declined.
- **Human time budget:** ~1 hour/day of editor time for promotion work.
- **Chosen approach:** search-anchored flywheel (owned reach as backbone) + social-native
  content formats + reader-tips community moat.

## Section 1 — Positioning & audience

**Positioning:** Predel News is *the* news source for Blagoevgrad and Southwest Bulgaria —
faster and more complete than anyone else, because the pipeline watches sources 24/7 and
publishes in minutes, with every article human-approved. Reader promise: „Ако се е случило в
Пиринско, вече е при нас."

**Audience layers:**

1. **Core — locals 30–60 on Facebook** — the existing Page + the ~28 regional groups. This is
   where regional news is consumed in Bulgaria today; the approved captions/scheduling design
   serves exactly this layer.
2. **Growth — under-35 locals on Instagram** — currently unreached; the newly approved
   channel. (TikTok/Shorts would extend this layer further — deferred 2026-07-18.)
3. **Passive bonus — the SW-Bulgaria diaspora** — heavy hometown-news consumers via the FB
   groups; served automatically by doing layer 1 well, no dedicated effort.

## Section 2 — Channel architecture: the flywheel

Everything points back to the website, because search is the only audience we own.

- **Owned reach (backbone):** Google Search + Google Discover. For Bulgarian regional outlets
  Discover is typically the largest single traffic source; it is currently untapped. Unlocking
  it is mostly one-time technical work (structured data, news sitemap, Publisher Center).
- **Social reach (amplifier):** Facebook Page (social captions + scheduled slots — already
  designed) and Instagram (automated posts via the extensible PublishJob). Short vertical
  video (TikTok / YouTube Shorts / Reels) is deferred (2026-07-18) — the flywheel runs on
  FB + IG + search until it is revisited.
- **Community reach (moat):** the ~28 groups (manual paste block already generated by the
  pipeline) + a reader tip line („Подай сигнал") that turns readers into sources — exclusive
  local stories no national outlet or aggregator can copy.

**The loop:** pipeline publishes fast → social + groups push it into the region → engagement
and repeat visits signal quality → Search/Discover rank the site for local queries → more
readers → more tips → more exclusive content → stronger rankings.

## Section 3 — Growth-feature roadmap (pipeline work, priority order)

1. **Ship the approved Facebook engagement design** (social captions + suggested-time
   scheduling — spec 2026-07-17). Biggest lever for the existing audience; already designed.
2. **SEO / Discover pack** (Predel-News Umbraco side, mostly one-time):
   - `NewsArticle` JSON-LD (headline, datePublished, dateModified, author, image);
   - news sitemap for fresh articles;
   - verified Open Graph tags;
   - images ≥ 1200 px wide (Discover requirement — the pipeline already fetches large stock
     photos);
   - Google Search Console verification + Google News Publisher Center submission.
   Zero ongoing cost; compounds forever.
3. **Instagram publishing leg:** add an `InstagramPublisher` destination to the
   multi-destination PublishJob (Graph API image post; caption reuses `facebookCaption` +
   hashtags). **Sequencing win:** the Meta app review already needed for the Page token (M6
   blocker) must request `instagram_content_publish` in the same review — one review cycle
   instead of two.
4. **Branded share-card generator:** server-side render of the headline on a branded
   1080×1350 template image. Used for IG when no usable photo exists, improves FB thumbnail
   CTR, doubles as the video title card, and is ADR-0009-safe (no licensing risk).
5. **Smart insights** (existing backlog item): FB/IG metrics polling → data-driven posting
   windows → trend-weight feedback. Build after the channels exist so there is data worth
   polling.

**Explicitly not building:** newsletter, Viber/Telegram channels (declined), automated video
generation (manual with templates instead), any paid-ad tooling.

## Section 4 — Editor playbook (~1 hour/day)

**Daily (~40 min):**

- Morning: share the top 2–3 articles to a *relevant subset* of the ~28 groups using the
  existing paste block — rotate groups, don't blast all of them with everything (group admins
  ban spammers; relevance keeps the account welcome).
- Reply to every comment on the Page within the first hour after posting — first-hour
  engagement is what the Facebook algorithm rewards most, and it is free reach.
- One Instagram story from the day's top article (share-card + link sticker).

**3× per week — DEFERRED (2026-07-18):** the short-video routine (CapCut template →
TikTok/Shorts/Reels) is not implemented for now. The reclaimed time goes to group sharing,
comment replies and stories.

**Weekly:** „Седмицата в Пиринско" recap post; pin the week's best performer on the Page.

**Monthly:** one partnership action — municipality, football club, school, hospital: offer
coverage, ask for a share/follow — plus a 30-minute KPI review.

**Burnout valve:** if time runs short the drop order is video first, stories second; group
sharing and first-hour replies are the last things to cut.

## Section 5 — Content strategy

- **Reach magnets** (post fast, schedule into peak windows): emergencies, weather, road
  closures, prices and jobs, local football, incidents (within „никога жълто"),
  infrastructure works. These get shared into family chats.
- **Engagement magnets** (the caption CTA does the work): questions and mini-polls,
  nostalgia/history photos of the region, "people of Pirin" features, reader photos. These
  build the comment habit that trains the algorithm.
- **Moat content — reader tips:** promote „Подай сигнал" (Messenger/phone) weekly on the
  Page. Tips become exclusive stories nobody else has; the existing editor `/post` verbatim
  flow already turns them into articles.
- **Cadence:** 3–5 FB posts/day inside the scheduler windows (config already supports this via
  `MaxPerDay`), 1 IG post + 1 story/day. (Video cadence deferred 2026-07-18.)

## Section 6 — Measurement (all free)

- **Site:** GA4 weekly users; Search Console clicks + the dedicated Discover report;
  search-vs-social traffic split.
- **Social:** FB follower growth and median reach/post (manual monthly until smart-insights
  ships), IG followers/reach.
- **North star:** total weekly unique reach (site users + FB reach). Baseline recorded in
  week 1, reviewed monthly.
- **90-day success bar (modest, realistic):** median FB post reach ×2 vs. baseline; site
  appearing in Discover; 500+ IG followers; ≥1 tip-sourced exclusive story/week.

## Section 7 — 90-day rollout

| Weeks | Focus |
|---|---|
| 1–2 | Ship FB engagement design; GA4 + Search Console baseline; submit the **bundled** Meta app review (Page token + `instagram_content_publish`) |
| 3–4 | SEO/Discover pack live; Publisher Center submission; editor starts group-rotation + first-hour-reply routine |
| 5–8 | Instagram leg live; share-card generator; tip line promoted |
| 9–12 | First partnership; KPI review → double down on what works; decide on smart-insights |

## Risks

- **Platform dependency** — mitigated by the search backbone (owned reach).
- **Editor burnout** — the 1 h/day cap and the drop-order valve in the playbook.
- **Meta review delays** — already the M6 blocker; bundling the Instagram permission avoids a
  second wait.
- **AI-content platform policies** — low risk: everything is human-approved original
  synthesis, no verbatim scraping (06-security.md).
